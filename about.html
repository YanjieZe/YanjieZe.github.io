About the Last Refugee

the Last Refugee is the personal blog of Yanjie Ze, and is named after the song of Roger Waters, The Last Refugee.

The purpose of this website is to share my thoughts typically in the field of programming and life, etc.

The source code of this website is public, you may find it here. You're welcome to open issues in the repo about your thoughts/comments regarding this website.

Oh, and, just in case you want to follow me on Twitter :)
